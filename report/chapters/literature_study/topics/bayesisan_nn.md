# Bayesian NN
jospinHandsOnBayesianNeural2022

BNN is a stochastic NN trained using Bayesian inference. Allows a NN to say how uncertain it is in its result. 

"Stochastic neural networks are a type of ANN built by introducing stochastic components into the network. This is performed by giving the network either a stochastic activation (Figure 3b) or stochastic weights (Figure 3c) to simulate multiple possible models i with their associated probability distribution p (i)."

"the main goal of a stochastic neural network architecture is to obtain a better idea of the uncertainty associated with the underlying processes."

"Second, a BNN allows distinguishing between the epistemic uncertainty p (i ; D) and the aleatoric uncertainty"
